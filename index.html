How Our Device Work?

Image Capture:

Use a high-resolution camera to capture detailed images of the eye, specifically targeting the corneal and anterior chamber areas.

Non-Contact Measurement:

Illuminate the eye with a controlled light source, such as infrared or LED, to measure the deformation or reflection caused by intraocular pressure without touching the eye.
